Privacy Policy

WhereIsMyMoney  ·  Last updated: April 2026

WhereIsMyMoney is a personal expense tracking app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

1. Who we are

WhereIsMyMoney ("the app", "we", "us") is developed and maintained by Vivek Goyal. For any privacy-related questions, you can reach us at vivekgoyal.vg29@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the app's features:

  • Phone number — collected during sign-in via Firebase Phone Authentication (OTP). Used solely to verify your identity.
  • Transaction data — amounts, categories, accounts, comments, and dates that you enter manually. Stored locally on your device.
  • Budget data — budget amounts and categories you configure. Stored locally on your device.
  • SMS messages — only if you explicitly grant SMS permission, the app reads bank debit SMS messages to extract expense amounts. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any server.
  • Camera — only if you use the QR code scanner feature to scan UPI payment QR codes. No images are stored or transmitted.
  • App usage data — we do not collect analytics, crash reports, or behavioural tracking data.

3. How we use your data

  • Your phone number is used only to authenticate you via Firebase and is not used for marketing or shared with third parties.
  • Transaction, budget, category, and account data is stored locally in a SQLite database on your device. It is not uploaded to any cloud service.
  • SMS data is read and parsed on-device only. It is never transmitted externally.
  • QR code data (UPI strings) is used only to open your chosen payment app and record the transaction. It is not stored beyond the session.

4. Data storage and security

All financial data (transactions, budgets, accounts, categories) is stored locally on your device using SQLite. It does not leave your device unless you explicitly export it as an Excel file using the in-app export feature.

Firebase Authentication is used for phone number verification. Firebase may retain authentication tokens as per Google's Firebase Privacy Policy.

5. Permissions we request

  • CAMERA — required for QR code scanning. Used only when you tap the QR scan button.
  • READ_SMS — required to load expense data from bank SMS messages. Only accessed when you explicitly tap "Load expense from messages". Never accessed in the background.
  • INTERNET — required for Firebase phone number authentication (OTP).

You can revoke any of these permissions at any time from your device's Settings.

6. Third-party services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — for phone number sign-in. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use any advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or third-party tracking tools.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties, except as required by law or as described in Section 6 (Firebase Authentication).

8. Data retention and deletion

All data stored locally on your device can be deleted at any time using the "Delete everything" or "Reset app" options available in the app's main menu.

To delete your Firebase authentication record, please contact us at vivekgoyal.vg29@gmail.com and we will process your request promptly.

9. Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
